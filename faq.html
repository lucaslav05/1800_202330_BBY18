Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How Can I Post A Bin?

A: To post your own bin, navigate to the main screen and press the post button.
then fill in all of the provided fields and press post


Q: How Do I Use Search Filters?

A: When in the Find a Bin page, use the checkboxes to search for bins
that accept the specified items


Q: How Can I See The Items In My Bin?

A: Navigate to the My Posts page, there you will see the number
of items in your bin


Q: How Can I Favourite A Bin?

Find Your Desired Bin Through The Search feature, then click the star
to add it to your Favourites


Q: How Do I Search By Location

A: When in the Find a Bin page, enter your postal code
and hit the Search button


Q: How Can I Delete My Post?

A: When in the My Bins page, click on the bin you wish to delete
click the ellipsis, then Delete button in the top right
