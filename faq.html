Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How Can I Post A Bin?

A: To post your own bin, navigate to the main screen and press the Post button.
Fill in all of the provided fields and press Submit.


Q: How Do I Use Search Filters?

A: Click Find a Bin on either the home page or in the hamburger menu to navigate to the Available bins. Click Filter by Item Type and select an item from the provided list. Then click Apply Filter to view the bins that accept that item type.


Q: Can I Let Others Know That I Have Recycled At Their Bin?

A: For sure! Navigate to the post for the bin that you recycled at and click Upload Picture. Click Choose File to select a picture to upload, and then cick Post Now to upload the picture.


Q: How Can I See What Others Have Recycled At A Bin?

A: Click View Photos at the bottom of a post.


Q: How Can I Favourite A Bin?

A: After clicking View Details on the bin that you would like to favourite, click the Add to Favourites button in the top right corner.


Q: Can I See The Location of a Bin on a Map?

A: Yes! After clicking View Details on the bin that you would like to see on a map, click the View on Map button on the top rigth side of the page.


Q: How Can I Delete My Post?

A: When in the My Posts page, click View Details on the bin you wish to delete. Then click Delete Post and then Confirm Delete.

Q: What Do "Active" and "Inactive" Mean?

A: When one of your posts says Active it means that others can see your post. When your post says Inactive, only you can see it.

Q: How Can I Make My Post Inactive

A: A post is automatically changed to Inactive when the end date of the post occurs. If you would like to manually change the status of your post to Inactive, click on End Event and then Confirm End.
